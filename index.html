About Me:

I am Sabi Woofington, 23 years old, and I am studying a degree in Information Technology Engineering in Mexico. I have certifications from Cisco Networking Academy.

I have knowledge in basic animation, digital drawing, pixel art, traditional drawing, video editing, music composition, programming, networking, databases, preventive and corrective maintenance in hardware and software, graphic design, cooking, and baking.

I am pansexual and currently in a relationship.

I enjoy video games, especially survival and open-world games, and I also love music!
